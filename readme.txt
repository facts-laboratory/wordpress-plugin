Deploy articles to the permaweb with the Permafacts Truth Market block. This customizable block lets you showcase articles and invite readers to vote on their accuracy, promoting transparency and accountability in journalism.

== Description ==

This block allows you to deploy articles to the permaweb and invite readers to vote on their accuracy through the use of for and against tokens.

Here's how it works:

Add the block to your page or post.
Publish the page or post to make the block visible.
As an admin, you will see a deploy screen in the block. Deploy the article to make it visible to readers.
As a reader, you will see a market screen with the article available for purchase.
Readers can buy for or against tokens to vote on the accuracy of the article.
== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

Upload the plugin files to the /wp-content/plugins/permafacts-truth-market directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use the Permafacts Truth Market block? =

To use the block, add it to your page or post, publish it to make it visible, and then deploy the article from the block. Once the article is deployed, readers can purchase for or against tokens to vote on the accuracy of the article.

= What is the permaweb? =

The permaweb is a decentralized and permanent storage network that allows you to store content forever.

= How does the Permafacts Truth Market promote transparency and accountability in journalism? =

The Permafacts Truth Market promotes transparency and accountability in journalism by allowing readers to vote on the accuracy of articles using for and against tokens. This provides a way to hold journalists accountable for the accuracy of their reporting.
